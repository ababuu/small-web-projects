Here's a Summury of the Emporer's life:

  • Born: 23 July 1892 Ejersa Goro, Harar, Ethiopian Empire
  • Reign: 27 September 1916 – 2 April 1930
  • Father: Makonnen Wolde Mikael
  • Mother: Yeshimebet Ali
  • Spouse: Menen Asfaw
  • Religion: Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo
  • Signature:
  • 1st & 5th Chairman of the Organization of African Unity, In office: 25 May 1963 – 17 July 1964
  • Regnal name:(native) Haile Selassie I (ቀዳማዊ ኀይለ ሥላሴ, qädamawi häylä səllasé), (English) "Power of the Trinity"
  • House: House of Solomon (Shewan Branch)
  • Died: 27 August 1975 (aged 83) National Palace, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
  • Burial: 5 November 2000 Holy Trinity Cathedral, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
